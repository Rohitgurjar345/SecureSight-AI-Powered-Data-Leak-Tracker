Project Overview

SecureSight is an AI-powered cybersecurity tool that helps users check if their personal data has been exposed and understand their risk level through actionable intelligence.

The Problem

Data breaches are constantly increasing. Users often don't understand their risk profile, and standard tools only show raw data dumps without providing analytical insights.

Our Solution

Targeted email breach detection combined with cryptographically secure password leak checking, culminating in a robust risk score and actionable mitigation recommendations.
